About

I build complete products, not just features.

From a single idea to a live, paying platform — I own the whole stack: a Node/Express + PostgreSQL backend, a Next.js admin panel, a React Native mobile app, and the Docker/VPS deployment that ships it all.

My flagship is ChatFleet — a multi-tenant SaaS that unifies Facebook, Instagram and WhatsApp inboxes, automates order-to-courier dispatch, and feeds real delivery outcomes back to Meta Ads to cut ad cost. I love the hard, unglamorous parts: zero-loss order pipelines, timezone-accurate analytics, payment integrations and secure webhooks.
